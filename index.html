Sumedh is famous for his role of Krishna in RadhaKrishn. Since 2018, Sumedh has been playing the role of Lord Krishna in Swastik Picture's magnum opus Radhakrishn which started on 1 October on Star Bharat.

Mudgalkar made his acting debut in Channel V’s Dil Dosti Dance, a dance-based youth show where he portrayed the role of Raghav aka Raghavendra Pratap. Then he became known for portraying the negative role of Yuvraj Sushim in the Indian historical drama Chakravartin Ashoka Samrat

Sumedh was nominated in the category of Best Actor for his debut film Manjha in the Filmfare Marathi Awards 2018.In 2019, he won the Indian Telly Award for Best Onscreen Couple & the Gold Award for Best Actor Debut from Radhakrishn.

In 2018, he featured in the Marathi music video "Bekhabar Kashi Tu" alongside Sanskruti Balgude.
